Plenty: The Flavor Farmers

Mechanical Design

Plenty's mission is to improve the lives of plants, people, & our planet. We do this through the production of delicious produce grown indoors. Indoor vertical farms create the perfect environment for plants, reducing the unpredictability of changing climates. No bleach or pesticides are needed. Just pure food that tastes like nature intended. Visit plenty.ag for more information.

As a senior mechanical design engineer at Plenty, I've been responsible for the design, development, and deployment of grow spaces, automation equipment, and plant containers that are actively running in our Bay-Area production farm. I've further developed and re-engineering some of those systems for our 2nd-generation farm currently being constructed in Los Angeles.
